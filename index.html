Afterlife is a solo project coming soon in 2023 to itch.io & Steam. Afterlife is a multiplayer ghost hunting game the has players investigate locations around the country that have been reported to have paranormal activity. Using equipment & team work, you must discover the ghost type that is haunting the location & figure out how you're going to remove it. The game offers different modes such as Career, Custom Game, Multiplayer & training. Afterlife was inspired by Phasmophobia which is available on steam.

Physics-World is a solo mobile game about physics based puzzles. Originally Physics-World started out as an assessment for a physics unit & eventally was remade into a 2D game, players control a bouncy ball that trys to find its owner after being lost. Going through different environments the player will need to use the environment around them to grab, slide, & launch themselves to the finish point! coming soon to Android, Physics-World featuring a 5 stage game full of 10-20 levels each with different & unique obstacle to avoid.

Those Nights at Randy's 5: Radiation is the fith game in the Those Nights at Randy's fan series of Scott Cawthons Five Nights at Freddys (2014 - 2022), all rights go to Scott. Radiation takes place sometime during the late 1970s, the player works as a new trainee at Randal Razberg & Friends Diner. Players will need complete different tasks during each shift to gain their full time position. The game offers a single-player story full of great horror aspects to create a scary environment.
